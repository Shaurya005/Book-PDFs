With forty (already seen), fifty, sixty, seventy, eighty and ninety
the numbers are build equal: Fifty-one, fifty-two, etc.
And then we have hundred, which could be a combination with the five
that is one hundred and five. With the ten: One hundred and ten.
With the twenty-one: One hundred and twenty-one. With the fifty-one
(which is already regular): One hundred and fifty-one.
