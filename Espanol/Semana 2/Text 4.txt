Hello, how are you? We are going to talk about the adjective.
How to form the plural of the adjective.
If the adjective ends in a vowel, we add an -S.
Mexicano, mexicanos - mexicana, mexicanas (Mexican)
Remember that in Spanish adjectives have both gender and
number. This is different to English.
For example, Mis amigos mexicanos/Mis amigas mexicanas (My mexican friends).
More examples: española/españolas (Spanish)
Estadounidense/Estadounidenses (From the USA). The Spanish teachers.
The students from the USA.
If the adjective ends in a consonant, we add the ending -ES
For example, español/españoles (Spanish),
alemán/alemanes(German)
For example, the Spanish sportsmen/women. The German tourists. In the plural.
